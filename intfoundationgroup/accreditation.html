Accreditation

  2. Accreditation

Accreditation Service for International Colleges (ASIC)

IFG London is accredited by the Accreditation Service for International Colleges (ASIC).

www.asic.org.uk

LanguageCert

LanguageCert is committed to developing and delivering high-quality, internationally recognised language exams that enhance the career, education and life prospects of individuals worldwide.

www.languagecert.org

Athe (Award for Training & Higher Education)

IFG Business Management, Engineering, Computer Science & Social Sciences Foundations are endorsed by ATHE, an Ofqual authorised awarding body.

www.athe.co.uk

Qulifi

The IFG Pre-Masters in Business Management Includes a Diploma in Business Management, Leadership and Innovation accredited at Level 6 by Qualifi, an Ofqual awarding body.

www.qualifi.net

NCFE

The IFG Medical Foundation is accredited by NCFE, an Ofqual authorised awarding body.

www.ncfe.org.uk

Trinity College London

IFG London is an accredited exam centre for Trinity College London English language exams www.trinitycollege.com

www.trinitycollege.com
